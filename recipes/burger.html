Burger

Description

Burger can be delicious if you right cook it. But do't eat burgers very often.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Minced pork
  • Ketchup
  • Bun
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Make cutlets from pork and fry
  2. Spread bun with ketchup
  3. Slice cheese and vegetables
  4. Set burger in order: bun, cutlet, cheese, vegetables, bun
