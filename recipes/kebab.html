Kebab

Description

Kebab is not only very yummy but so healthy food if right cooked. Let's make it!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Vegetables
  • Ketchup
  • Cheese
  • Pita

Steps

  1. Fry chicken on tyni fire
  2. Spread pita by kethup
  3. Slice chicken and put it into pita
  4. Add cheese and vegetables
  5. Wrap pita that you can hold it
